Video Project Title: Coconut - The Inspiring Journey

Project Description

This short documentary tells the heartwarming story of Coconut, a small dog whose resilience and spirit captivated an online audience, quickly amassing over 1 million views. The video aimed to highlight the importance of animal rescue and the profound impact animals can have on our lives. Its viral success led to its adaptation into a national TV commercial for the ASPCA, further amplifying its message.

Role & Contributions

As the Director and Editor for this project, I was responsible for the entire creative execution, from initial concept development and storyboarding to filming, interviewing, and all aspects of post-production. Key contributions included crafting a compelling narrative structure, capturing emotive footage, and editing the piece to maximize emotional impact and shareability, all while working closely with the ASPCA team.

Behind the Scenes (Optional)

The production involved several on-location shoots to capture Coconut's daily life and interviews with those closest to her. One of the challenges was working with an animal actor, ensuring Coconut was comfortable and natural on camera. The overwhelming positive response from viewers was incredibly rewarding.
